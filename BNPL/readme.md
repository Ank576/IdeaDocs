
# BNPL Module Documentation

This folder contains documentation, design artifacts, and strategy for building a Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) service integrated into an e-commerce platform.

---

---

## Strategy Overview

### Business and Customer Focus
- Target the right customer segments.
- Promote BNPL benefits like increased conversion and order value.
- Define a commercial model (in-house lending vs partner financing).

### Product & User Journey
- Seamlessly surface BNPL offers at checkout stages.
- Ensure transparency with clear disclosures.
- Provide easy post-purchase management and reminders.

### Credit & Risk
- Use eligibility rules plus advanced credit scoring.
- Employ fraud detection layers.
- Set dynamic transaction and credit limits.

### Technical Architecture 
- Implement API-driven BNPL services.
- Ensure modular design for easy integration.
- Include robust monitoring and failure handling.

### Compliance & Legal
- Align with lending and consumer protection laws.
- Document consent and data policies.
- Develop legal agreements and audit trails.

### Finance & Operations
- Clarify merchant settlement and revenue sharing.
- Reconcile accounts with clear processes.
- Prepare operational playbooks for disputes and collections.

## Usage

Use this folder as a blueprint for BNPL development, covering end-to-end aspects from problem definition to implementation, compliance, and analytics.

Feel free to expand or adapt documents to your organization's needs.
